Privacy Policy for TikTok Automation Bot

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our automated application handles user data. Our application is a personal automation tool designed strictly for content management and educational purposes.

Data Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, share, or process any personal data from our users or any third parties.

API Permissions

The application utilizes TikTok API scopes (such as video upload and basic profile access) solely to facilitate the direct transfer of the user’s originally created video files to their personal TikTok profile. No data obtained through these APIs is recorded, cached, or transferred to external servers.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact the application developer directly.
